Our Mission is to Empower Africa's Talent.

Erudio was founded in Lagos with a simple yet powerful idea: talent is everywhere, but opportunity is not. We saw countless brilliant minds held back by the traditional barriers of education. We set out to change that, creating a platform that connects passionate instructors with eager learners, no matter their location or background.
